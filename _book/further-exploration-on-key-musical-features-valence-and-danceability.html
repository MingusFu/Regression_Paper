Chapter 3 Further Exploration on Key Musical Features: valence and danceability

To gain an in-depth understanding of the results for Hypothesis 3 (H3), an OLS regression analysis was conducted between danceability and track popularity. This was done in light of various existing evidence that supports the notion that people prefer tracks they can dance or move to, in relation to their reasons for listening. (e.g., Duman et al. 2022; Loepthien and Leipold 2022)

reg_2<-lm(track_popularity~danceability,edm)
